Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FindMEDKH

General
What is FindMEDKH?▼
FindMEDKH is a hospital recommendation platform for Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Describe your symptoms in plain language, and our chatbot matches you to the right medical specialty and recommends the nearest hospitals instantly.
Is FindMEDKH free to use?▼
Yes — completely free. Guest users get 5 free chatbot messages. Simply create a free account for unlimited access. We never charge for basic hospital search.
Which hospitals are listed?▼
Over 60 public and private hospitals and clinics in Phnom Penh — including Calmette, Sunrise Japan Hospital, all Kantha Bopha children's hospitals, and many specialist clinics for dental, eye, mental health, and physiotherapy care.
Is this a replacement for medical advice?▼
No. FindMEDKH is a hospital finder — not a medical diagnosis tool. Always consult a qualified doctor for medical advice. In an emergency, call 118 (ambulance) or go directly to the nearest ER.
Chatbot
How does the chatbot recommend hospitals?▼
The chatbot uses keyword-based intent detection — no heavy AI required. It tokenizes your message, scores keywords against known medical categories, picks the best specialty, and queries our database for matching hospitals sorted by GPS distance.
Why only 5 free messages as a guest?▼
Guest sessions are temporary. Registering a free account removes this limit entirely and lets us save your chat history so you can continue conversations later.
Can I get hospitals sorted by distance?▼
Yes! Click the "Enable Location" button in the chatbot sidebar and allow location access. Hospitals will immediately be sorted nearest-first using GPS coordinates.
What languages does the chatbot support?▼
Currently English only. Khmer language support is planned for a future release.
Account & Privacy
Is my health data private?▼
Yes. Chat messages are only stored when you're logged in, and only you can view your history. We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data. Passwords are securely hashed with bcrypt.
How do I delete my account?▼
Currently an admin can delete accounts. Self-service account deletion is on our roadmap.
I forgot my password — what do I do?▼
Password reset via email is on our roadmap. For now, please register a new account. We apologize for the inconvenience.
Hospitals & Data
How often is hospital data updated?▼
Our admin team reviews and updates hospital data regularly. If you notice incorrect information, please contact us so we can fix it promptly.
Can I suggest a hospital to be added?▼
Absolutely. Reach out to our admin team and we'll review the request. We're actively expanding our database.
What does "24/7" mean on a listing?▼
It means the hospital has 24-hour emergency services available. Always call ahead to confirm for non-emergency care as staffing can vary.
