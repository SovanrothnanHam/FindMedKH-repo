Our Mission

Healthcare Access for Everyone

In Phnom Penh, finding the right hospital for your condition can be confusing — especially in emergencies. FindMEDKH was built to change that.

We believe everyone deserves fast, clear guidance to the right medical care. Our chatbot maps your symptoms to the right specialty and the nearest hospital in seconds.
