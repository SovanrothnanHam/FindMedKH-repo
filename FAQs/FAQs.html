Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions About FindMedKH — Answered

How does FindMedKH recommend hospitals?

Posted on February 20, 2026

Our system analyzes your symptoms and matches them with medical specializations in our hospital database. The AI chatbot then recommends hospitals that best fit your needs and location.

Is the chatbot a real doctor?

Posted on February 18, 2026

No. Our chatbot is an AI assistant designed to provide hospital recommendations based on symptoms. It does not replace professional medical advice from licensed doctors.

Is FindMedKH free to use?

Posted on February 15, 2026

Yes. FindMedKH is completely free for users searching for hospital recommendations and medical information.

Can I search hospitals by location?

Posted on February 12, 2026

Yes. You can search hospitals based on city or nearby location, helping you find the most convenient healthcare provider.
