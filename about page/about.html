About FindMedKH

Revolutionizing Hospital Discovery in Cambodia

Who We Are

FindMedKH is a modern healthcare technology platform built to simplify how people in Cambodia search for hospitals. We combine intelligent systems with medical data to ensure patients connect with the right healthcare providers faster and easier than ever before.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce confusion during medical emergencies by providing accurate, fast, and AI-powered hospital recommendations based on symptoms, specialization, and location.

Our Vision

We envision a smarter Cambodia where technology bridges the gap between patients and healthcare services — empowering people with reliable information at their fingertips.
